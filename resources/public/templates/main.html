Go local for books!

Type in the book you want. We'll show you which bookstore near you has it in stock.

We're book lovers, plain and simple. And we heart bookstores. We love browsing through staff picks, brushing elbows with fellow readers, stumbling upon new authors on the shelves.

But too many bookstores are struggling to keep their doors open. Well, we wanted to change that. So we created an easy way for people to source books locally. We are in beta right now so if you have any feedback, please click the feedback button to e-mail us.
